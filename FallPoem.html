Fall

I sit outside.

The clouds shifting.

The trees swaying in the wind.

The wind blowing against me.

The leaves falling.

The sound of cars passing by.

The sun peeking through the trees' leaves.

The peaceful sound of the wind blowing against the trees.

The leaves seeming to blow away from me.

My cold fingers.

The moment when all your problems seem to fade away.

When all you can think about is the beautiful view you're witnessing.

Holding this one leaf that seemed to have blown my way.

This one thing that I will now cherish.
